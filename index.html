About Remo Energy

Your Trusted Energy Partner

Founded in 2023 in Kampala, Uganda, Remo Energy was born out of a vision to transform the energy landscape in Uganda and beyond. Our mission is to deliver sustainable, reliable, and affordable renewable energy solutions that empower communities and drive economic growth.

We understand the critical role energy plays in every society — and we're committed to making clean energy accessible to everyone, particularly in regions where traditional power is unreliable or expensive.

Our Vision

To be the leading renewable energy provider in Uganda, empowering communities with the power of the sun.

Our Mission

Delivering innovative, customised, and safe renewable energy solutions that meet the diverse needs of every customer.
